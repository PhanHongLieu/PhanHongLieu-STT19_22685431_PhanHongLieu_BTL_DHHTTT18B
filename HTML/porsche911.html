Porsche 911 Carrera S

1. Engine

  • Engine Type: 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine
  • Power Output: 443 horsepower (331 kW)
  • Torque: 390 lb-ft (529 Nm)

2. Horsepower

    443 horsepower at 6,500 rpm, which allows the car to accelerate from 0-60 mph (0-100 km/h) in approximately 3.5 seconds when equipped with the Sport Chrono package.

3. Performance:

  • City: 18 mpg (13.1 L/100 km)
  • Highway: 25 mpg (9.4 L/100 km)
  • Combined: 21 mpg (11.2 L/100 km)

4. Features

Performance Features:

  • Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM)
  • Sport Exhaust System
  • Porsche Torque Vectoring Plus (PTV+)
  • Rear-wheel drive (optional all-wheel drive in Carrera 4S)

Interior:

  • Leather upholstery
  • 14-way power-adjustable seats
  • 10.9-inch touchscreen infotainment system (Porsche Communication Management, PCM)
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • Optional premium sound system (Bose or Burmester)

Safety and Driver Assistance:

  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Keep Assist
  • Front and rear parking sensors
  • Forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking
  • Optional Porsche InnoDrive with adaptive cruise control

Safety and Driver Assistance:

  • LED headlights
  • 20-inch front and 21-inch rear wheels
  • SportDesign Package (optional)
  • Rear spoiler with active aerodynamics
