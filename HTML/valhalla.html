Aston Martin Valhalla

1. Engine

  • Engine Type: 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid
  • Horsepower: 1,000 hp (combined engine and electric motor)
  • Torque: 1,200 Nm (883 lb-ft) of combined torque

2. Fuel Economy

  • Combined Fuel Consumption: Estimated around 12-14 L/100 km (due to hybrid powertrain)
  • Electric Range: Around 15-20 km (electric-only driving range)

3. Features

Performance:

  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) with hybrid assist
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): Approximately 2.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 330 km/h (205 mph)

Safety & Driving Assistance:

  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Traction Control: Advanced traction control system
  • Driving Modes: Sport, Track, and Hybrid

Interior & Comfort:

  • Seats: Carbon-fiber sports bucket seats with adjustable settings
  • Interior Materials: Premium leather and Alcantara upholstery
  • Entertainment: High-end audio system, infotainment screen with advanced navigation
