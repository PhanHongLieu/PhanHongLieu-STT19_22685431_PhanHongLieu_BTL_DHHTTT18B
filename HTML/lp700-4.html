Lamborghini Aventador LP700-4

1. Engine

  • Engine Type: 6.5L V12, Naturally Aspirated
  • Horsepower: 700 hp @ 8,250 rpm
  • Torque: 690 Nm @ 5,500 rpm

2. Fuel Economy

  • Combined Fuel Consumption: Approximately 15-16 L/100 km
  • Highway Fuel Consumption: Around 11-12 L/100 km
  • City Fuel Consumption: Around 20-22 L/100 km

3. Features

Performance:

  • Transmission: 7-speed ISR automated manual transmission
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): Approximately 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 350 km/h (217 mph)

Safety & Driving Assistance:

  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Traction Control: Advanced traction control system
  • Driving Modes: Strada (Street), Sport, and Corsa (Track)

Interior & Comfort:

  • Seats: Sports seats with electric adjustments
  • Interior Materials: Premium leather upholstery
  • Entertainment: High-quality audio system, infotainment screen
