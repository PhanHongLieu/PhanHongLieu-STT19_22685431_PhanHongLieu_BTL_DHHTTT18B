Porsche Cayenne

1. Engine

  • Engine Type: 3.0-liter V6 (Hybrid available)
  • Power Output: Up to 541 horsepower (403 kW)
  • Torque: 567 lb-ft (770 Nm)

2. Performance:

  • 0-60 mph: 3.9 seconds (Cayenne Turbo)
  • Top Speed: 178 mph (286 km/h)

3. Features

Performance Features:

  • Porsche Dynamic Chassis Control (PDCC)
  • Active all-wheel drive

Interior:

  • 14-way power-adjustable seats
  • Heated and ventilated seats
  • Touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay

Safety and Driver Assistance:

  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning
  • Park Assist with Surround View Camera
