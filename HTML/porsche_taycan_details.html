Porsche Taycan

1. Engine

  • Engine Type: Dual electric motor setup
  • Power Output: Up to 616 horsepower (454 kW)
  • Torque: 626 lb-ft (850 Nm)

2. Horsepower

    616 horsepower, capable of accelerating from 0-60 mph (0-100 km/h) in as little as 2.6 seconds with the Taycan Turbo S.

3. Performance:

  • City: 80 MPGe
  • Highway: 90 MPGe
  • Combined: 85 MPGe

4. Features

Performance Features:

  • Instant torque delivery
  • Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM)
  • Sport Chrono Package

Interior:

  • Premium leather upholstery
  • Curved 16.8-inch digital display
  • Adaptive cruise control with Porsche InnoDrive

Charging:

  • DC Fast Charging (up to 270 kW)
  • Up to 60 miles of range in just 5 minutes of charging
